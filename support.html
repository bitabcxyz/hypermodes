Frequently Asked Questions
73| 74|
75|
How do I create an account?
76|
Download the Hypernodes app from the App Store, open it, and tap "Sign Up". You can register with any email address — no special invitation code needed.
77|
78| 79|
80|
Is my data secure?
81|
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
82|
83| 84|
85|
How do I delete my account?
86|
You can delete your account directly from the app settings (Profile → Settings → Delete Account). Alternatively, email us and we'll process the request within 48 hours.
87|
88| 89|
90|
Can I change my email address?
91|
Yes. Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Change Email. You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect.
92|
93| 94|
95|
I found a bug — how do I report it?
96|
Please email us at support@hypermodes.itseek.dev with a description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and your app version. We'll investigate promptly.
97|
98| 99|
100|
What should I do if I forgot my password?
101|
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password". We'll send a password reset link to your registered email address.
